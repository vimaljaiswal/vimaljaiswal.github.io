About Us

Appsnack helps brands and their agencies build rich engaging app-like creative experiences that reach targeted audiences at scale, across mobile and tablet.

Appsnack is a division of Exponential Interactive, the global digital advertising company that also owns global display advertising provider Tribal Fusion, engagement video platform Firefly Video and in-stream video advertising specialist AdoTube.

Exponential is headquartered in Emeryville, California and offers solutions to brand advertisers in 26 countries.
